The Difference...

Raven B+, is built upon Raven B3, but there are many differences between them both. Raven B+ has almost double the modules of Raven B3, and it has even improved modules that existed in B3, such as the autoclicker module.

It has new modules, such as AutoHeadHitter (A module which spams space bar while under a block to make your hits criticals), Bridge Assist, LSDfov, Fun modules, Hotkeys, and config loader!


Is Raven B+ malware?

Raven B+ is open source, you can check for yourself, it's available on GitHub, and you can even build the .JAR yourself. It is trusted by over 500 people so far. Everything is a risk nowadays.
