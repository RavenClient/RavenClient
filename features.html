Features

Raven B+, is so customisable, and can sometimes be very hard to configure, or make sense of a module. Below is a breakdown of every module and how to use! As Raven B+ is constantly being developed, this list will update to keep up with the modules, so check back if you need any help.


Combat


Auto Clicker
    Automates the task of clicking.
  • Left clicker: Automatically attacks for you when left mouse button is held. Ranges from 0-60 CPS.
  • Right clicker: Automatically right clicks for you when right mouse button is held. Ranges from 0-60 CPS.
  • Inventory Fill: Autoclicks inside the inventory GUI for shift clicking. Handy for pot PVP refilling.
  • Weapon only: Only autoclicks when the player is holding a weapon.
  • Prefer Fast Place: Uses Fastplace instead of Autoclicker for the right mouse button, when Fastplace is toggled.
  • Break blocks: Allows the autoclicker to break blocks when aiming at a block, instead of autoclicking. Ranges in delay times from 0ms - 1000ms with randomiser.
  • Allow eat: Allows the player to eat when holding an item of food, instead of autoclicking.
  • Allow bow: Allows the player to draw back a bow, instead of right autoclicking.
  • Jitter right/left: Jitters the mouse slightly when autoclciking to imitate a legits gameplay. Handy for streaming.
  • Click Style: Raven uses the autoclicker from basic RavenB3, whereas Skid uses a different type of autoclicker pattern.
  • Event: Render shows your cps on the keystrokesmod, as you have set it in the autoclicker. Tick, pretends your only clicking around 11 cps, but in reality is clicking what you set it as. Handy for streaming.

Aim Assist
    Helps aiming at enemies within a certain distance.
  • Speed 1 & 2: Allowing the user to choose how fast the aimassist can move to an enemy, adding some randomness between the speed.
  • FOV: Will only aim at an enemy if they are within a set FOV, making you seem more legit.
  • Distance: The distance at which it will aim at an enemy from. 10 blocks is very far.
  • Click aim: Will only aimassist when clicking.
  • Break blocks: Will not aimassist to a player if you are trying to break blocks.
  • Ignore freinds: Will not aimassist towards freinds that you have set. Handy for team games.
  • Weapon only: Will only help aiming when you are holding a weapon.
  • Aim invis: Will help aiming at people that are invis.
  • Blatant mode: Will lock on to the enemy with no hesitation. Very blatant.

Burst Clicker
    Will imitate drag clicking patterns.
  • Clicks: You can choose your drag click CPS from 0-50.
  • Delay: How long the drag click is held for, imitating long or short drags.
  • Delay randomiser: Adds randomisation to starting delay and length of drag click hold. Basically to seem more legit.
  • Place when block: Instead of drag clicking with your left mouse button, it will place blocks when holding a block. Good for godbridging legit.

Click Assist
    Helps your CPS out. :D
  • Chance: The chance at which it will add a click in amongst your own.
  • Left/right click: It will help you out with both mouse buttons.
  • Only while targeting: Will only add clicks in when you are aiming at a player.
  • Blocks only: Will only increase your cps for rioght click when holding blocks. Handy for non repetetive block hitting while legit.
  • Above 5 cps: Only increase your cps when you are clicking above 5. Handy to stop double clicks when not wanted.
  • Weapon Only: Will only boost your CPS when holding a weapon.

Delay Remover
    Gives you 1.7 hitreg. Good with autoclicker.

Hitbox
    Increases the size of players hitbox, meaning easier to hit.
  • Multiplier: The size the players hitbox will be expanded.

Reach
    Gives you that sneaky reach advantage.
  • Hit through blocks: Gives hits through blocks. Dont use. Very blatant.
  • Weapon only: Will only give reach hits when holding a weapon.
  • Min/max: Uses different reach hits within the min and max to seem more legit.
  • Moving only: Only gives reach hits if moving.
  • Sprinting only: Only give reach hits if sprinting.

Rod Aimbot
    Aims your rod at the enemy within your FOV and distance. Good for rod PVP.
  • FOV: The fov you want the player to be within before activating.
  • Distance: The distance you want the player to be within before activating.
  • Aim Invis: Rod Aimbot at invisible players.

Velocity
    Reduces knockback of your player when hit.
  • Horizontal/Veritcal: Choose your knockback with these two sliders.
  • Chance: The chance of you taking less KB. Seems more legit.
  • Only while targeting: Only give you less KB when fighting another player.
  • Disable when holding S: Disables antiKB when holding S key.

WTap
    Taps the W key, when you hit a player to give them more knockback.
  • Min/Max Ms: The length that it holds down W before releasing.
  • Once Every Hits: It will only W Tap every selected hits, so not to over-do the WTapping.
  • Range: How close the player has to be to WTap.

STap
    Taps the S key, when you hit a player to prevent them from hitting you, and resetting your sprint.
  • Min/Max Ms: The length that it holds down S before releasing.
  • Once Every Hits: It will only S Tap every selected hits, so not to over-do the STapping.
  • Range: How close the player has to be to STap.

Blockhit
    Blocks your sword when you hit a player, to reset your sprint.
  • Min/Max Ms: The length that it blocks your sword before releasing.
  • Only When RMB: It will only blockhit when the RMB is held down.
  • Once Every Hits: It will only Blockhit every selected hits, so not to over-do the blockhitting.
  • Range: How close the player has to be blockhit.

ShiftTap
    Shifts for a brief moment after you hit a player. This sprint rests your character, meaning you deal more knockback to your oponent.
  • Min/Max Ms: The length that it shifts for before releasing.
  • Once Every Hits: It will only shift every selected hits, so not to over-do the ShiftTapping.
  • Range: How close the player has to be ShiftTap.

AutoWeapon
    Automatically switches to the best weapon in your hotbar when you hit a player.
  • Only When Holding LMB: Will only AutoSwitch when you are clicking the LMB.
  • Revert To Old Slot: Will switch back to the old slot in your hotbar once you stop holding LMB.



Movement


BHop
    Increases player movement speed.
  • Speed: How fast it will increase your speed by.

Boost
    Increases the tick speed of the game for a short time.
  • Multiplier: How fast it increases the tick speed by.
  • Time: How long it boosts it for.

Fly
    Makes your player, well, fly.
  • Value: Toggle between two modes, glide and vanilla fly.
  • Speed: How fast the fly speed is.

Inv Move
    Allows you to move while in menu's such as chests.

Keep Sprint
    Allows the player to keep hitting while sprinting.
  • Slow %: How much it reduces your sprint speed by on hits.

No Slow
    Reduces movement slowdown when it water, cobwebs etc...
  • Slow %: The amount it speeds you up when you should be slowed.

Speed
    Speeds up the player movement speed.
  • Speed: The amount it speeds it up by.

Sprint
    When W is pressed it will sprint for you.
  • Omnisprint: The ability to sprint in other directions.

Stop Motion
    When toggled, will stop all movement and reset the X,Y and Z player speed variables.
  • Stop X/Y/Z: Stop the players movement in these directions.

Timer
    Increases the game speed, player movment speed, and alorund game.
  • Speed: The amount it increases the game tick speed by.
  • Strafe Only: Only increase the games speed when moving using A or D keys.

VClip
    Teleports the player up/down depending on the value put in by the user.

Auto Head Hitter
    Presses the space bar when underneath a block to make your hits criticals.
  • Jump Presses /sec: How many times it presses SPACE per second.



Render


Anti Shuffle
    Shows a players true name when the server disguises it with random characters.

Chams
    Renders a players model ONTOP of everything else, showing players through walls.

Chest ESP
    Highlights chests or storage containers through walls.
  • RGB: Change the colour of the chest highlight.

Player ESP
    Shows players through walls.
  • RGB: Change the colour of the outline.
  • 2D / Arrow / Box: Different ways of outlining the player.
  • Health: Shows the players health next to them.
  • Ring: Renders a ring around them, showing you how far they can attack from.
  • Shade: Shades in the ESP box.
  • Show Invis: ESP's with invisible players.

Tracers
    Draws a line from your crosshair to each player within your render distance.
  • RGB: Customize the colour of the tracers.

HUD
    Shows the current modules that are toggled on in a list.
  • Logo: Dispays a logo above the module list.
  • Value: Change the looks of the module list.

XRay
    Outlines ores through walls and floors.

FullBright
    Basically night vision all the time, but better.

CustomFOV
    Allows you to set a custom FOV from 0-420.
  • FOV: The custom FOV amount.



World


AntiBot
    Prevents modules such as AimAssist and PlayerESP from interacting with bots spawned in by server anticheats.

Chat Logger
    Logs all chat messages into a TXT file for you to review.



Client


Client Name Spoofer
    Spoofs the name of the client in F3 mode.
  • To use: Send a command in the "Command Line" formatted like this: f3name [Name]

Command Line
    Opens the command Line where you can send commands. Use "help" to see a list of commands.

GUI
    There are 3 mods of GUI you can choose from.

Self Destruct
    Stops the functions of B+ from working. Only use when you dont need the cheats anymore. This is automatically binded to "Backspace".

Update
    Opens the Download Link in the broswer for you to download the latest version.
