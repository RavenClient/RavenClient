Features

The main features in Raven B+. These are ranging from simple visual hacks to advanced hacks which make you undetectable. As raven gets updated more features will be added. The feature list below is not all the hacks, but some of the more useful ones.





Features

Auto Clicker Automatically clicks.
Reach Allows you to hit enemies from further away.
Hitbox Makes the enemies bigger.
Aim Assist Helps you aim by moving the mouse towards the enemies.!
Delay Remover Gives 1.7 hitreg.
Velocity Reduces the amount of knockback you take.
ClickAssist Has a chance to double clicks.
Chams Allows you to see people through walls.
Bed Aura Automatically breaks beds nearby. (detectable)
Burst Clicker Simulates drag clicking.
Duels Stats Uses the hypixel api to display the stats of other players
Sumo Fences Puts a wall around the sumo arena to prevent getting hit out.
FastPlace / AutoPlace Places the blocks for you when you hold right click.
Safe Walk Prevents you from falling off the edge of a block.
Speed Makes you move faster.
PlayerESP Makes a box around players which you can see through walls.
Auto Combo W-taps/S-taps/Blockhits Automatically for you to put the enemie into a combo.
Bridge Assist Aims for you when bridging.
